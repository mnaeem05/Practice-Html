Chemistry

The branch of Science concerned with the substances of which matter is composed, the investigation of their properties and reactions, and the use of such reactions to form new substances. There are some basic formulas like :

Carbon Dioxide: CO2
Water: H2O
Sulfur Dioxide: SO2
Sulfuric Acid: H2SO4

M. Ali Jinnah
